Mohamed Wanko's 90-Day Daily Reminder

Reset. Rebuild. Reclaim.

Phase 1: RESET (Days 1–30)
  • Daily: 5-min silence, 10-min journaling, 10-min reading, 5-min intention
  • Remove: Gossip, distractions, draining talk, guilt cycles
  • Weekly Check-In: What gave me life? What drained me?
"Peace begins when you stop arguing with your soul."
Phase 2: REBUILD (Days 31–60)
  • Daily Identity: Declare: "I am the kind of man who ___."
  • Creative Action: One small bold step, daily
  • Passion Practice: 30 minutes creating daily
"You don't find purpose. You build it by showing up with truth."
Phase 3: RECLAIM (Days 61–90)
  • Set & Speak Boundaries: Write 5, speak daily
  • Step into Mentorship: Share 1 truth weekly
  • Truth Reflection: Where was I true? Where did I shrink?
"You don’t have to be loud to be powerful. Protect your peace with quiet fire."
